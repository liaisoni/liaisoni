The Process


1. Fill in the contact form with your contact information and a brief (100-200 word) paragraph explaining what's on your mind (idea, vision, goal etc). Make sure to include any sources of inspiration (text/photo/video/etc) for your brand.

2. Wait for a reply from us and then we can schedule a one-on-one to discuss and explore your options.

3. Send us your tech pack or team up with one of our partner designers and perfect your design.

4. Request samples and tweak your design with fit models. Discuss order size, order fulfillment and ecommerce store setup.

5. Receive your shipment and start selling!
